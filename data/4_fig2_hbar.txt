We identified the top 10 emerging jobs as those with the highest CAGR among all 814 occupations from 2017 to 2021. Robotics Engineers are at the top of the list with 48.89% compounded annual growth since 2017, although it remains with a low number of available jobs with under ten jobs posted in 2021. Other occupations in the top 10 include a few roles that also appeared in LinkedIn's 2020 Emerging Jobs [list](https://business.linkedin.com/content/dam/me/business/en-us/talent-solutions/emerging-jobs-report/Linkedin_EJR_PH_final.pdf), although some have a slight difference in naming, such as Cyber Security specialist to our Information Security Engineers.
